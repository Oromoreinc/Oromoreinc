1. Overview

InSaver is a social video downloader for HarmonyOS. It allows users to paste a video link and save the video directly to their device gallery. InSaver also includes a built-in web browser to assist users who need to access content that requires authentication. This Privacy Policy explains how InSaver handles information in relation to your use of the app.

InSaver does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device and has no connection to any external server operated by Oromore Inc.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

InSaver does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Login credentials or account information for any platform
  • Browsing history or activity within the app
  • Downloaded video files or media content
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Usage statistics or analytics data

3. Video Downloads

InSaver's core functionality is simple — you paste a video link, and InSaver downloads the video directly from the source platform's servers to your local device storage. Oromore Inc. does not have access to, receive copies of, or store any downloaded content on any external server. All downloaded content remains entirely on your device.

4. Built-in Web Browser

InSaver includes a built-in web browser that allows users to access social media platforms directly for the purpose of browsing and downloading videos. If you choose to log in to any platform within the built-in browser, your login credentials are entered directly into that platform's official website and are handled entirely by that platform's own systems.

Oromore Inc. does not have access to, intercept, or store any login credentials, passwords, or session data. All authentication is managed solely by the respective platform.

5. Permissions

InSaver requests the following device permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Internet Access: Required to fetch and download videos from source URLs.
  • Network Information: Used to check connectivity status before initiating downloads.
  • Write Media (Gallery Access): Required to save downloaded videos to your device gallery.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit your data to Oromore Inc. or any third party.

6. Third-Party Services

InSaver supports video links from Instagram, a service operated by Meta Platforms, Inc. Your use of Instagram is subject to Instagram's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. Oromore Inc. is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or in partnership with Meta Platforms, Inc. or Instagram.

The app may display advertisements served by third-party ad networks. These ad networks may use cookies or similar technologies in accordance with their own privacy policies. Oromore Inc. does not control these third-party ad services.

7. Children's Privacy

InSaver is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since the app collects no data whatsoever, no data from any user of any age is stored or processed by Oromore Inc.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Oromore Inc. reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
