1. Overview

Instatube is an Instagram-focused browser application for HarmonyOS that allows users to browse Instagram and save videos directly to their device. This Privacy Policy explains how Instatube handles information in relation to your use of the app.

Instatube does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device and has no connection to any external server operated by Oromore Inc.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Instatube does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Instagram login credentials or account information
  • Browsing history or activity within the app
  • Downloaded video files or media content
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Usage statistics or analytics data

3. Instagram Login

Instatube includes a built-in web browser that loads Instagram's website directly. If you choose to log in to your Instagram account within the app, your login credentials are entered directly into Instagram's official website and are handled entirely by Instagram (Meta Platforms, Inc.).

Oromore Inc. does not have access to, intercept, or store your Instagram username, password, or any session data. All authentication is managed solely by Instagram.

4. Video Downloads

Videos that you choose to save using Instatube are downloaded directly from Instagram's servers to your local device storage. Oromore Inc. does not have access to, receive copies of, or store any downloaded content on any external server.

5. Permissions

Instatube requests the following device permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Internet Access: Required to load Instagram in the built-in browser.
  • Network Information: Used to check connectivity status.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit your data to Oromore Inc. or any third party.

6. Third-Party Services

Instatube loads Instagram, a service operated by Meta Platforms, Inc. Your use of Instagram within the app is subject to Instagram's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. Oromore Inc. is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or in partnership with Meta Platforms, Inc. or Instagram.

The app may display advertisements served by third-party ad networks. These ad networks may use cookies or similar technologies in accordance with their own privacy policies. Oromore Inc. does not control these third-party ad services.

7. Children's Privacy

Instatube is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since the app collects no data whatsoever, no data from any user of any age is stored or processed by Oromore Inc.

Oromore Inc. reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
